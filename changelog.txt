audio.py
--------------------------------

27/02/26
Refactor to follow Single Responsibility Principle (SRP).

RunSweep - Conductor that manages audio streams and execution of the other audio processing classes.

DSPUtils - A utility class containing pure mathematical functions such as Hann windowing, dB-to-linear conversion, and cross-correlation.

MarkerGenerator - Responsible for creating the Barker-13 sequence used to synchronize the timing of the recordings.

SweepGenerator - Handles the creation of the sweep signal and its corresponding inverse filter.

HarmonicInjector - A testing class that adds H2 or H3 harmonic distortion to the sweep signal to verify Farina distortion separation capability.

ProtectionFilter - Applies a high-pass filter (either Minimum or Linear Phase) to the playback signal to protect speaker drivers from damage.

AlignmentEngine - Manages temporal alignment of multiple sweeps by detecting the Barker markers and performs averaging to reduce noise.

DeconvolutionEngine - The core engine that performs deconvolution, applies spectral masking (DC and Nyquist minimum-phase filters), and separates the linear response from distortion.
